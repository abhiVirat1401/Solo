About us

Nurtured from the seed of a single great idea - to empower the customer - Solo is a pioneer World’s designers industry. The company initiated its journey serving the US-India online market offering a range of best-value products and services powered by technology and round-the-clock customer support.

Solo has proactively diversified its product offering, adding a variety of online services. Solo has stayed ahead, continually evolving its technology to meet the ever-changing demands of the rapidly developing global online market, steadily establishing itself as India’s leading online travel company.

Mission

Solo's main objective is to design websites, portfolios, online markets for our valuable customers. We are confident with our service and our mission is to make customers happy.

Vision

With online marketing at high demands all over the world, we wish every seller, trader and entrepreneur to have their own stunning websites and an oppurtunity to grew up business.

Passion

Solo is dedicated to its work. We never allows our customers to complain. Customers satisfactory is our prime motive. We are confident and passionate to our work.
